What is Sudoku ?

Sudoku is a logic-based, combinatorial number-placement puzzle. In classic sudoku, the objective is to fill a 9×9 grid with digits so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3×3 subgrids that compose the grid contains all of the digits from 1 to 9. The puzzle setter provides a partially completed grid, which for a well-posed puzzle has a single solution.




How to play ?

After you click on the Start playing! button, you will be able to upload a picture of the sudoku you want to solve. After uploading it, you will be asked if there was any error in the process of recognizing the numbers of the grid, in which case you can correct them. Then you can start playing ! A timer will be started and you can start solving the sudoku, and when you think you are done, you can submit your result and get your score. Have fun !
